# SAHR Diagnostic Reasoning Test Dataset

This dataset is designed to stress-test a symbolic reasoning engine's ability to perform:

- causal reasoning
- multi-cause diagnosis
- evidence-based filtering
- recovery reasoning
- hypothesis elimination

The dataset contains statements (knowledge) followed by questions with expected answers and reasoning chains.

# Statements (Knowledge Base)

Reaction wheels control spacecraft orientation.

Reaction wheels contain a wheel motor.

A wheel motor failure causes the reaction wheel to fail.

A reaction wheel failure causes spacecraft orientation control to fail.

Spacecraft orientation control failing causes spacecraft instability.

Thrusters control spacecraft orientation.

Thrusters are powered by propellant.

Reaction wheels are powered by electrical power.

Magnetorquers are powered by electrical power.

Temperature spikes occur before wheel motor failure.

Temperature spikes indicate impending wheel motor failure.

Telemetry indicates temperature spikes.

Telemetry indicates electrical power remained available.

Electrical power remained available.

Propellant remained available.

Thrusters restore spacecraft orientation.
